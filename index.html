Welcome to nature!

In recent years the trend of adventuring out in nature has gone up in popularity. Especially in my homecountry of Sweden.
One of the largest reasons was of course for the more relaxed restrictions under the pandemic which made nature available when many other things were not.
The other big reason is that we have a certain law in place called "The Right Of Public Access".
I myself use much of my sparetime visiting and living in nature recreationally. That's where I come in, this will serve as an introduction for outdoor life in Sweden!
