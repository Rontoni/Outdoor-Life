Canoeing

Now this is an outdoor activity people engage with in many ways!
From canoeing to kayaking and paddling around the coast or following a river.
The advantages if you for example use a canadian canoe are quite a few.
You can take a heavier load of equipment and supplies with you,
making it so you can be gone for a couple of days without needing to refill.
One thing to note, allways use a life jacket.
Accidents can happen and you don't need much time under water before you have drowned
